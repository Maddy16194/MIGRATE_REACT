Materials & Shipping Info

Looking for something bigger? Custom picture frames for any photo.

Museum-Quality in Every Frame

  • Your photo is giclée printed using archival inks guaranteed not to fade
  • Each frame is handcrafted in the USA using wood profiles and acid-free, 4-ply matting to preserve your memories over the long haul
  • Premium, framers grade Acrylite® plexi chosen for its strength and clarity
  • The mat window opening is 5" x 7", outer frame dimensions are 9" x 11"
  • Built into the frame is an easel for standing on any flat surface, as well as sawtooth hardware for hanging on the wall
  • Gift wrapping options at checkout. Save money when you buy more than one!

Shipping Info

Each tabletop framed photo is handcrafted to order and shipped to your door in about a week. Please note any deadlines you may have in the Special Instructions field at checkout. You will receive an email with tracking info when your order ships. If you have any questions about the process, just let us know via chat or at hello@levelframes.com.

We Stand by Our Work

Our goal is to help you on this framing project and many more to come. If your frame shows up with any defects or you’re not completely satisfied, let us know and we will make it right.
